Woocommerce multiple gift registry plugin allows your customers to create gift registries for special occasions and invite their friends and family to purchase items from the registry. Woocommerce multiple gift registry plugin tracks all items purchased and the quantities remaining.

The person who created the registry can add products to it from the product detail page. The store administrator can view and share customer gift registries. To access a gift registry, recipients can click on a link that is shared with them via Facebook, Twitter, or by scanning a QR code. Registry visitors who want to make a purchase can add the item to their carts directly from the gift registry.

Gift Registry Workflow

The customers create their own registries. A customer creates a new gift registry by going to their store account and completing the required fields in each section of the gift registry. After adding items to the registry, it can be shared with friends and family.

Invitations are sent out with a link to the gift registry attached.

If you receive an invitation, you can order any item from the registry
